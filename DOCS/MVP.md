# MVP Product Planner - BillTrack (Updated)

## Problem

People lack understanding when it comes to bills, banks, and money. Specifically, they struggle with:

- Understanding how bills work (due dates, payments, types)
- Tracking and managing their budget effectively
- Remembering when bills are due

## Core Benefit

BillTrack is an interactive bill tracker that simplifies financial literacy by helping users visualize their bills, understand what they owe, and receive timely reminders so they never miss a payment.

## Core Features

1. **Visual Bill Tracker** - Add and manage bills with clear status indicators (Paid, Due Soon, Overdue, Upcoming) organized by category
2. **Budget Summary Dashboard** - See total monthly bills, paid amounts, remaining balance, and progress at a glance
3. **SMS Reminder System** - Save phone number and enable text reminders for bills so users get notified when payments are due

## User Flow

1. User enters their phone number for reminders
2. User adds their bills (name, amount, due date, category)
3. User enables/disables SMS reminders per bill
4. User marks bills as paid to track progress
5. User views budget summary to understand their financial obligations

## Success Metric

Users can accurately track and categorize at least 3+ bills with reminders enabled, reducing missed payment stress and increasing financial awareness.
